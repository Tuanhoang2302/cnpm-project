 *

 * var music = this.sound.add('key');
 * music.on('loop', listener);
 * music.setLoop(true);
 * ```
 *

 * 
 * @param {(Phaser.Sound.WebAudioSound|Phaser.Sound.HTML5AudioSound)} sound - A reference to the Sound that emitted the event.
